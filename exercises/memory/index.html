Internet's First Memory

What was your first memory of the internet?
My first memory of the internet is not one single momnet. It was completely absent from my life and then all of a sudden my memories were flooded with it. It was present in school classrooms, tv shows I watched, and conversations with my family. In the span of a few years, it surrounded me completely and now it is hard to think of a memory that has not been impacted by the internet.
Some of the specific early memories I have include:
  • Zoombinis Game
  • AIM
  • Google Drive for Class
